COMMUNITY CENTER

Users can exchange opinions and information such as promotion of their work, job postings, collaboration projects, and event announcement, etc., in this community space that will be introduced through the closed beta service.

MY GALLERY

Users can display their work, decorate with objects to their preference, and interact with visitors in this personal space.

PROJECT ROOM

Users can conduct various meetings, lectures, and small group activities. Various functions specialized for content creation can be used. Also, creative collaboration with continuity and expertise is possible through data storage system.

NFT STAKING CENTER

Through NFT stacking, users can deposit their investment for a certain period of time and receive a fixed return.

NFT AUCTION CENTER

A Space where NFTs with proven value are traded. Users can buy quality NFTs made by investment in CREVERSE or NFTs of verified artists through an auction. NFTs receive a share of copyright revenues through staking system.

ARENA

A space where various types and scales of performances and contests to discover promising creators are held.

ART SCHOOL

A space for creators to learn. Users can attend lectures by content professionals from all over the world and the lectures allow free communication between them.

CREATOR CITY

A place that will be released in the official service. This city is a new type of space for creators to come together and it can be expanded unlimitedly around Main Street. Creators decorate empty buildings to their heart’s content. As those personalities come together, the city will develop in unexpected ways.

CREATOR SUPPORT CENTER

A space for getting information necessary for creative activities. Creators can receive copyright-related legal/tax advice, contract notarization (NFT issuance of contracts). In addition, various news and information related to content creation are provided.

WORK STATION

A space that provides different types of tools for creators in CREVERSE. Necessary tools for editing/recording/VFX/3D/Graphics work are provided.
